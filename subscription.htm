Subscriptions


Contents

Overview

 

The subscription model enables you to expedite your tasks, send bulk email and text messages while simultaneously complying with privacy regulations.

A subscription will be added when preparing to send a mailshot, e.g. an event invitation, newsletter, or other communication.

 

Topics

 

Features

•Only contacts added to the subscription (subscribers) will receive the communication.

•They can easily opt out.

•Multiple subscriptions can be added for various categories including events, newsletters, general notifications, etc.

•New contacts will automatically be added for future communications.

 

Add

Go to Initiatives > Subscriptions > Add new subscription.

•Add the name of the subscription and comments.

•Select Internal for internal communication groups.

•Select Online to make the subscription available online for contacts to opt in or out.

•Select Save and stay on page to add subscribers, or Save and close.

 

 

Add contacts

Contacts can be added here, or via the mailshot.

•To add individual contacts, select Subscribers > Add one subscriber.

oSelect Save and stay on page to add additional comments.

oSelect Save and new to continue adding another contact.

 

You can add a group of subscribers by selecting one of the following:

•using the search page

•using the reporter

•using the importer

See Search & report in the online help to view details of how to use these features.

 

View subscriptions and statistics

•Select Initiatives > Subscriptions > View subscriptions to see a list of:

•your existing subscriptions

•the number subscribed

•how many are unsubscribed.

•Select Statistics to extract a report showing how many have opted out by month.
