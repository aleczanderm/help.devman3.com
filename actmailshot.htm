Mailshots


Contents

 

By creating a mailshot you can email or SMS many contacts in one session at the same time.

 

In this topic we discuss how to create a mailshot and how to compose and send the message.  

 

You can also import contacts from a spreadsheet and send these contacts an email or SMS.

 

We also discuss the basic email newsletter template which has been designed so that a user can fully customise the look and feel of their newsletters.  

 

Finally we show you how you can track your mailshots using the delivery reports in the system.

 

If you haven't used this facility before there are some SMS and email settings that must be made first. Contact your are administrator or other person who has the needed permissions to get it done.

       

A very useful facility in an email is to have a way to get the recipient to respond by clicking on a link in the email.

Your email can be an invitation to an event and they RSVP or it can be a request to update the database via online updates.
