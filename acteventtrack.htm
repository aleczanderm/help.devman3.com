Manage an event


Contents

 

1.Once you have sent invitations to your guests, you can manage the details of your event in the Manage guest list screen.

 

2.First you'll need to find your event. Go to Events > View events.  

 

View events

 

 

 

3.Select your event, by clicking on the appropriate row in the grid.  In the Event details screen, click Manage guest list.

 

Events summary page

 

 

 

 

4.In the Manage guest list screen, you can Add more guests to your event, and send emails or SMS.  

 

Manage an event

 

 

 

 

5.You may wish to indicate who you have invited by ticking the invited block in the appropriate row. However to tick all guests as having been invited, click Update > Invited.  The same would apply should you wish to tick all as having accepted, declined, or attended.

 

Click here to view

 

 

6.By clicking Update > Not invited / accepted / attended, you can untick all guests.
 

7.You can select a guest by ticking the selected block next to their name.  Alternately, if you wish to select a group of people, perhaps all who accepted or attended. Click Select > Accepted / Declined.  Once selected you can SMS or email these guests, or print a report.  

 

Click here to view

 

8.If you wish to delete a guest from your list, select a row in the grid, and hit the Delete key.

 

9.Perhaps you would now like to print a guest list, or name tags.
