Overview

 

As interactions are recorded on the system, you are notified in the Weekly activity section of the Home page:

You may view these new interactions by clicking on this notification.

To  see older interactions go to View a past interaction

You can go add an interaction directly via communications or from a contact. See how to add an interaction.

 

Add an interaction via communications or contact

1. Click on "Communications > Interactions > Add >

2. Open the contact or project and click on Add > Activity > Interaction.

 

Enter a description and other details.

 

 

Change other details

If necessary, change the date. You can also change the person, organisation or project name, by using the relevant drop-down lists.  

 

 

Save and close

Once you have captured all the relevant information, you can Save > Save and close, or Save as.
