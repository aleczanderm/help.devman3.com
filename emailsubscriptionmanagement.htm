WHAT IS IT

Devman in collaboration with GraphicMail provides email subscription management. GraphicMail is a leading provider of specialised bulk emails and electronic newsletter services.

 

WHY YOU NEED IT

Send better email – You focus on engaging emails and newsletters and let the specialists manage the complexity.  Everyone who sends bulk emails will soon run into spam filter issues - GraphicMail ensures your mail gets to your audience, and is designed to take care of the restrictions imposed by the POPI act on subscription management.

 

HOW IT WORKS

While DevMan continues to be your core operational system to manage beneficiaries, donors and mailing lists, there’s a direct link to GraphicMail, the tool to help you create and send beautiful email newsletters.

•You can create professional newsletters from templates (400 available); or design your own,

•check your spam score before sending

•verify your email addresses

•monitor the mailshot stats

 

Steps:

•Set up your mailing list on DevMan and manage your subscriptions

•Once set up, a link from your selected mailshot group on DevMan will take you to the GraphicMail site

•Create your email / newsletter template

•Check your spam score

•Send

 

COSTS

If you are a registered NPO you may qualify for 10 000 free credits per month (1 credit = 1 address).  For other organisations, credits are purchased on the GraphicMail site from 2c per email.  Verifying of email addresses is done once off at a cost of 15c per 1000 addresses up to 100 000 addresses.

 

Contact DevMan support to set up your GraphicMail integrated account.
