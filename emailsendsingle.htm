Single email

 

1.If needed search for the contact (make sure your contact has an email address), tick your selection and click Send and choose Email.

 

2.Enter the details such as the From address and Subject line.  
You can type your message directly into the block or you may choose a template (see Mailshot templates.)

 

3.Once you're satisfied with your email and the address is valid select Send.

 

4.Select Communication > Email > View to check status of items sent.  If you wish to send an email to a group of people see create a mailshot.

 

Email someone directly

 

There are a number of ways to see if your SMS or email has been delivered.

 

1.Once you have sent your email or SMS you will see a new option on the menu bar. Go to View > Contact log or View > Contact log detail

 

2.The Contact log will indicate if your message was pending, submitted, sent or failed.  The Contact log detail provides email address, date and time. TIP

 

3.Once you leave this screen, you cannot go back  to it, however you can check if your emails or SMSs have been delivered by going to Administration > Contact log > and choosing one of the options.
 

By choosing Individual items you can see the individuals whom you SMS'd and emailed.
