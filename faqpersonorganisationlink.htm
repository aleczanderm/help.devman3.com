Linking persons to an organisation


Contents

Can more than one person be linked to an organisation?

Yes it is possible to have more than one person linked to an organisation.  

There can however, be only one primary link.

Here's how to go about adding additional persons to an organisation:

How to link a person to an organisation

 

1.Search on DevMan to see if the Organisation already exists on DevMan – either from the Home page or from the Organisations Page.

-Remember that using the Search from the main pages will give you the top 20 results that match your search criteria.

2.Your next step would be to see if the Person exists on DevMan as well.

3.If you find the Person on Devman – simply open the Person page and in the Organisation field type the company name – Save and close that record. The person is now linked to the organisation.  You will follow this process with linking more people to the same organisation – provided they already exist on DevMan.

4.If this is a new Organisation, you will capture as many of the details you have for the organisation.  You will also need to have the Person Info on hand when creating a new Organisation. Once you satisfied that you have entered all the details you have on hand, you can then Save and Stay on page, and the following message should appear:

 

 

5.You have now successfully created the organisation page with the first person link as the Primary Contact.  Your Primary Contact will be the person you decide is either the Key Decision maker or the one person you would contact in regarding whatever your requirements are for that organisation.

6.If the second person does not exist on DevMan – you will have to go to the Person Page and Create a new record and link it to the organisation by simply typing the newly created company name in the organisation field.

7.If the person is on the database – you will use your left hand menu and Add a new link to the organisation.

 

 

8.On the link page, you will notice the organisation name is already completed. You can  now just type the person Surname, Name in the person field, and then select from the drop-down list. A new person can also be added from this page by using Add. You will then Link this person to the organisation by ticking the box.  Save and close.

 

 

-You now have both persons linked to the same organisation.  On the organisation summary page, you will see the people who are linked to the organisation – at a glance!
