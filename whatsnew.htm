New features in DevMan

 

Event registration made simple

•Introducing our cutting-edge registration tool complete with automated workflows.

•Manage events on the go and streamline the registration process using mobile technology.

 

Online debit order sign-up

Donors can now complete an electronic debit order instruction via your donation page.

 

Subscription menu and dashboard have been moved.

You will now find the menu and dashboard for subscriptions under the Communication menu.

 

SARS 2023 section 18A receipt requirements

•Effective 1 March 2023, SARS requires additional information to be included in section 18A receipts issued to donors by approved section 18A institutions.

•DevMan is designed to assist you to gather this information via an automated self-service portal.

•To learn more, please see SARS 2023 section 18A receipt format below or contact the support team.

 

Notification refresh

•Weekly activities and pending actions will be refreshed every morning.

•If you would like to check  your progress as you work, you can refresh the notification count by clicking in the refresh button. (See image below)

•The new Refresh button is available on Home, Initiatives / Development, Communication and Finance dashboards.

 

Payfast

•DevMan integrates to the Payfast online giving gateway.

•This enables donors to choose from a variety of safe and secure payment methods and accept instant payments from all over the world.

•Please contact Support for more information.

 

Enterprise integration

•DevMan now offers three authentication topologies enabling seamless single sign-on integration.
