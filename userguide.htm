Institutional Advancement summary guide

This is a summary of the Devman features for institutional advancement and a link to notes about the feature.

Contacts - goto working with contacts

Alumni,donors, prospects and all other stakeholder - whether organisations, households and individual persons, intelligently linked in a hierarchical structure to give you a complete picture of relationships

Search and Lookup - goto search for a contact or advanced search

Simple searches display results dynamically as you type, extended and advanced searches allow filtering to group data for a report, dashboard or bulk action.

Interactions - goto interactions

Record every interaction with your contacts categorised - meeting, phone-call, em-mail, text messages, newsletters and events.

Approaches - goto Fundraising

Researchers identify and gather information about prospects for fundraisers when cultivating and approaching donors in a work-flow cycle.

Communications - goto Activities

Complete set of tools including bulk email, text messages and letter printing using personalised templates.

Projects - goto working with projects and proposals and proposals

All donations are allocated to a project. Projects can be structured in a multi-level hierarchy.

Pledges and Donations - goto working with contacts

Pledges and donations can be flagged in a rich variety of categories enabling flexible analysis of funds raised in dashboards, reports and to export to spreadsheets

Tax Certificates - goto working with contacts

Institutions qualifying can issue tax certificates for all donations either immediately for each donation or consolidated and printed annually.

Mailshots - goto working with contacts

in the box mailshot function where you can manage target audience, import lists from external sources. Create mailers with personalised graphics and response links to track responses.

Events - goto event activity

Complete functionality to manage and coordnate event planning, handle guest lists, send out invitations, track responses and other invitee details.

Dashboards - goto working with contacts

Interactive (dynamic) and graphic displays of your important data with dynamic filtering and drill-down to the underlying transactions.

Reports - goto working with contacts

Devman comes with  a rich suite of reports including summary, statistical contact, project, approach and finance. All reporting functions
