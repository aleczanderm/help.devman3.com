To quickly get you up and running we have this tutorial near the top of the table of contents (TOC).

It outlines the essential tools from the landing page through all the high level pages.

 

Landing page

 

Topics

 

Home page

Enter you user name and password  to get to the Home page. The home page will have most of these parts:

•My to do list is a list of items due for your attention over the next seven days and is visible only to you.

•Weekly activity is a summary of all activities for the last seven days.

•Pending actions are items that require attention by all on your team.

•Favourites are saved shortcuts to pages you were working in.

•Dashboards are high level views of key statistics. The drop-down menu provides a rich list of predefined views.

See DevMan functions for details on the various tabs.

 

Menus

There are three core menu structures:

•Tabs across the top provide access to key areas of DevMan.

•Sub-menus display on the left relative to the Tab you have selected.

•Drop-down menus are available wherever you see a small arrow on the sub-menu.

 

Personal settings

Manage the following from the My settings sub-menu:

•View and clear Recent notices of failed emails / document downloads.

•Manage shortcuts to your Favourite profiles.

•Adjust your Preferences for the number of gridlines, weekly email notifications and summary page view options.

•Add additional Email identities to your profile.

oMake sure to add the email address to your person profile.

oOn the Home page, select My settings > Email identity.

oComplete the email verification step to activate.

•Change your password.

 

Breadcrumbs, bookmarks and favourites

Breadcrumbs provide a trail of where you have been working.  One click takes you to a page you had open earlier. Hover the cursor over the breadcrumb to see where it will take you.

 

You can create bookmarks / favourites in DevMan to provide quick access to profiles you work on frequently.

Summary and edit pages

Toggle between summary / edit mode on relevant pages.

•Summary gives a high-level static view of key information.

•Edit is a detailed view where you can change (add/edit/delete) the content.

 

Saving your work

There are two options from the Save menu:

•Save and stay on page to save work done and then continue with further changes. The page will refresh and may show additional fields.

•Save and close when you have completed your entry. The summary page will now display.

 

FAQ

       How do I manage my bookmarks or favourites?

On your Home page, select My settings > Favourites. This will display a list of all your saved favourites.

Click on the row you want to change to view the pop up box, and there you can update the name of the link, or delete it.

 

       How do I go back to the previous page?

•Use the breadcrumb trail to go back to a previous profile.

•To navigate back to the previous page, use the DevMan back button and not the browser's back option.
