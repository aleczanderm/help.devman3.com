Saved selections

Save the selection

 

 

To create a saved selection:

➢Make your selection with the advanced search

➢Enter a name for the selection in "My selection"

➢Devman saves the selection

➢Click reset if you want to do a new selection

 

 

To edit a saved selection:

➢Choose your selection from "My selection".

➢Change the criteria as desired

➢Devman saves the selection

 

 

 

To copy a selection:

➢Choose your selection from "My selection".

➢Click "Save as"

➢Alter the name as desired

➢Devman saves the newly named selection.

 

All saved selections are linked to your log in only unless you select the Public checkbox

 

make the saved selection available to other users but other users will not be able to change or
