Add a payment

 Top 

If you have pledged money to a project, when making a payment it is essential to allocate the payment to the pledge, otherwise any financial or budget reports that you generate will be incorrect.

 

If you have not added a pledge, and wish to capture a payment directly to a project, you can simply add a payment:

 

1.Search for your approved project.

 

2.In the Project summary screen, go to Add > Payment > Once off. To split the payment into installments, you can click Payment > Installment plan.

3.Alternatively, in the project summary screen go to View > Financials > Payments made.

 

Click here to view

 

 

4.Click on New. To split the payment into installments, click Add installment plan.

 

Click here to view

 

 

5.In the Payment details screen, choose which budget owner the money is be paid from, the Budget period and category. (Once you have selected this information, each time you capture a new pledge the system will default to these choices, unless you change them).  In the description field you may include any information you feel is relevant to the pledge.  

 

6.Enter a date, and the amount you have approved for this project.
 

7.You can categorise your payments by Payment type e.g. Cash, Non-cash products & services, In kind, Discounts, vouchers etc.

 

8.You can indicate what payment method you will use e.g. EFT, cheque.

 

Click here to view

 

 

9.Once you have captured your payment information, choose Save > Save and close.  The Finance summary on the Project profile page will show the project as paid, and the Project status as Fully funded or Partially funded.
