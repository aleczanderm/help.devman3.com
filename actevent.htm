Events


Contents

 

Devman's Event management module simplifies this difficult and time-consuming job. This built in tool takes the trouble out of the entire process.

 

By using mailshot templates, you can send attractive, colourful personalised invitations to your guests.  Specific invitation and seating card layouts with your company logo on can be designed and incorporated for you by the Kidz Africa Team.  

 

Once you have invited your guests you can manage those who have accepted, declined and attended.  

 

Using Development Manager's reporting function you can print various types of guest lists and name tags.

 

Depending on your organisation's requirements you may wish to allocate parking, reserve seats, include partners etc.  You may also want to include dietary requirements.  If activated, the dietary requirements flag is available on the person profile page, and you can print a dietary requirements list via the Manage Guest List screen.  

 

If you would like to use any of these features, please discuss with your Kidz Africa Relationship Manager.

 

This Help sections provides help on how to add an event, manage it and print out a guest list and names tags.
