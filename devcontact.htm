Overview

 

This section describes how to work with your contacts on DevMan.

Your contacts are organisations as well as the people you deal with.

 

Features
Phone number formatting

•DevMan automatically validates and formats phone numbers based on the requirements for each country (as defined internationally). This ensures that all  SMSs sent via DevMan do not fail. For example:  US phone numbers get dashes, UK numbers get spaces, etc.

•Local numbers are saved in the format in which you would dial them, automatically removing the prefixed country code.  Should you prefer to have local numbers saved in the international format please contact support to enable. 

 

Email validation

DevMan ensures that you have valid email addresses.

See how to validate an email address.

 

Change log

Select Maintenance > Change History on the person profile summary page to view:

•what changes have been made to a person's email address and phone numbers.

•who made the changes.
