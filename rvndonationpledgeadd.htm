Add a pledge (fundraising)


Contents

 

Pledges and donations must be allocated to a specific project.  If you receive a large number of general donations to be used at your discretion, we suggest you create a project called General Donations or something similar (perhaps include the year), and allocate your general pledges and donations to this project.

 

Before capturing a pledge or a donation for a project ensure that your donor's details are on Development Manager. See Add a person or Add an organisation.

 

1.Search for and select your project.  

 

2.In the project summary screen, go to Add > Pledge > Once off.

 

Click here to view

3.Alternatively, in the Project summary screen, first view the pledges already made. Go to View > FInancials > Pledges

 

Click here to view

4.Click on New.  If the donation is split over a period of time, click Add installment plan.

 

Click here to view

 

5.In the Pledge details screen, type the Donor name into "Paid from" and select donor from the drop-down list.

 

Click here to view

 

 

6.Include a description and type in the amount. If you would like to send a consolidated tax certificate check the box.  See Print a tax certificate for more information.

 

7.Make your selection of the following:

 

Donation type - products or services are donated, not just money.

CRM (your organisation's) region - this is the region that was captured on the organisation (donor) profile. By reporting on this, you can analyse where most of your donations are coming from, whether nationally, regionally or internationally.

You can track which fundraising mechanism generated the most donations, by choosing a mechanism such as general donations, a specific event or mailshot.

 

8.Once you have captured all of your information, click Save > Save and close.

 

9.When you receive the money, you can pay the pledge.
