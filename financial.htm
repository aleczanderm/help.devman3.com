Managing financials


Contents

The management of financials is a core component of both Grant-making and Fundraising.

 

Whether you are doing grant-making or fund-raising Devman can transfer financials to your GL accounting system.

Data between the two systems is this in synch. Cuts down on work and helps to reduce errors associated with double entry.

 
Grant-making

Amounts requested in the proposals received, recommendations made and approved and tranches paid. In some cases the grants may be an in-kind donation (training or other expertise or physical goods) rather than money. Devman no longer requires that a monetary value be given to in-kind donations.

 

Provision is made for you to record these against your own grant-making budget (macro-budget). Once a project is approved you can allocate the funds by adding a pledge, and once the payment has been made, pay the pledge or add a payment.

 

Fundraising

Amounts requested in the proposals received, recommendations made and approved and tranches paid. In some cases the grants may be an in-kind donation rather than money. Devman does not requires that a monetary value be given to these in-kind donations.

 

Provision is made for you to record these against your own grant-making budget (macro-budget). Once a project is approved you can allocate the funds by adding a pledge, and once the payment has been made, pay the pledge or add a payment.
