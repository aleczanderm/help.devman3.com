Progress report scheduling procedures and flow

 

After the board meeting approvals are captured and a grant number is generated. A summary list is printed for the finance office (‘Grant commitment list per focus area’). Aita captures these details into the SUN Ledger manually including Devman generated grant number. Doing it manually is the preferred way as suggested by Alan Johnson for OSF CPT and ISISA JBG. Note the SUN ledger code is part of the DevMan focus area on this report.

Payments are automatically scheduled, during the approval process, by the wizard. Next is the payment ‘release‘ which will pop-up when due (the program keeps track of date). This allows the project officer to release the next payment. It is so important to capture payee details when entering the organisation details and allowing this to filter through to the payments. If you don’t do this you will need to do it on the payment form itself. The system does provide you to see those with incomplete payee details – see Project Management – Projects with missing payee details

 

Demonstrate payment release from pop-up and show the e-mail generated. Mention too that it will also pop-up for the Finance officer when she next goes into the system. Batch process is not enabled but can be if required.

Pop-ups augment the facility already provided to see payments due for release, payments to be made and completed payments under the ‘Finance’ sub-menu. (Pop-ups have just been enabled on their system – last week by James).
