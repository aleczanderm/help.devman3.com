Overview

 

Batch or bulk processing is the ability to process multiple (1 - to any number) records at once without tediously having to go through each record one by one.

Easily apply a single set of adjustments (e.g. status or flag update) to a group of records and carry on with other jobs.

 

Bulk processing is available on these record types:

➢contacts - person and organisation records

➢bursaries

➢projects

➢finance - pledge and payment records

 

The steps are the same for all the record types:

Select the record type to work with on the Main menu

Select the group of records.

Choose the update action.

Setup and do the bulk action..

Bulk action in progress and confirmed
