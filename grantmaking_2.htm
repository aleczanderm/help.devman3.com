Bursary management


Contents

The administration and management of a bursary programme can be labour-intensive and time consuming.

Devman's easy to use yet comprehensive bursary module reduces the challenge.

This guide presents the steps in a typical bursary life-cycle from application to  completion.

Suggestions for beginners

The central entity of the bursary student system is a Person record, from which all details about the person are linked.

These include the personal contact and bursary details and finances, study program and institution, academic achievements, sponsor, activities and interactions and dossiers

Overview - Bursary and Qualification

Bursary student  administration include:
- personal contact details

- qualifications & exam results

- financials
- work-flow tracks progress.

- bursary holder communications.

- students submit results directly online.

 

A person record is created for each student with their contact details.

A bursary record exists for each student for each study year.
