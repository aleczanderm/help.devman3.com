Pay a pledge (grantmaking)

 Top 

If you have pledged money to a project, when making a payment it is essential to allocate the payment to the pledge, otherwise any financial or budget reports that you generate will be incorrect.

 

If you have not added a pledge, and wish to capture a payment directly to a project, you can simply Add a payment.

 

1.Search for your approved project.
 

2.Go to View > Financials > Pledges.

 

Click here to view

 

3.Click on the pledge you wish to allocate the payment to on the grid.

 

Click here to view

 

 

4.In the Pledge details screen, choose Action > Pay Pledge.

 

Click here to view

 

5.If you are paying the full amount, choose Yes when prompted.  If you are only paying a portion of the full amount choose No.

 

Click here to view

 

6.The payment details screen is very similar to the pledge details screen, and includes all the information you require.  You may wish to change the date. If you have not flagged the pledge as fully receipted, change the amount.  You can Save > Save and close.  The Finance summary on the Project summary page will show the project as paid, and the Project status as Fully funded or Partially funded.

 

Click here to view

 

7.You may wish to print a tax certificate or Thank you letter.
