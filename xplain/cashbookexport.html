Cashbook exports

Download donation and payment transactions from DevMan and upload to your accounting system.

In this example, we will download donations received. 

Go to Finance > Cashbook exports > Transactions due.

Select the date range you wish to export and Search.

Number of transactions found.

To view individual transactions, select View transactions due.

To proceed with the export, select Process transactions due then Begin process.

Once the status shows Successful, click through on the Cashbook export and open the batch.

Select the Finance period from the drop-down menu.

Save and stay on page, then update the Status to completed.

Select Action then Export.

Status now shows Exported.

Click on OK to download.

Find the exported document in your Downloads folder.

Import it to your accounting system.
