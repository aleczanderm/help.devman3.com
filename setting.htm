 Top 

There are three levels in which Devman can be configured:

1.Feature settings made in the beginning to suit your organisation 's way of working.

2.Global or system-wide look and feel preferences and environmental settings

3.User or personal settings and preferences that affect you only

4.Settings and preferences for the new user

5.At times you may want other changes and enhancements.
Should you require Devman to be customised please speak to your Devman representative,
or call our support desk.
