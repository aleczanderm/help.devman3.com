Merge Contacts

 Top 

When you perform a search, you may find that you have two records for the same person or organisation.

In order to have good, clean data, it would be advisable to merge the two records.

By doing this you will be combining their history, contact details, activities, projects and financial data onto one record.

Once this is done, the obsolete contact can be deleted.

 

How to merge a duplicate person or organisation.

Open each profile to ascertain that they are in fact duplicates, and check the details such as initials, first name, surname, contact details, etc.

If you are certain that they are duplicate records, check to see which profile has the latest up to date contact information.

Open the older profile, and select Maintenance > Merge. This record will become the obsolete one.

Enter the name in the search box, and select the other organisation (or person) profile.

The profile will display in the right hand column and once it is open, you will be able to see the slight differences.

In this example, the contact person is different.

Compare the two records to confirm that the one on the right in the Correct info column is the correct one that you want to keep. Then select Merge.

 

 

Once DevMan confirms that the records have been merged, you can open the obsolete record, and delete it.
