Reports and exports


Contents

 

Whether you're wanting to print a report or extract and export data there are a few basic steps:

1.First a selection of the data (filter) you want to report on,

2.Choosing what information (layout) you want in the export or report.

3.When applicable there may be an option to order (sort) the data.

4.You may often want to print your selection in a batch instead of singly one by one.

Kinds of reports and layouts:

Devman provides report layouts for persons, organisations, projects, financials, budgets and activities.

Where appropriate reports can be displayed in a variety of graph types such as bar-charts and pie-charts

Additionally there is a facility where the report can be exported to spreadsheet for more options.

1.List data presented in columns - Some reports are presented in rows and columns (such as a list of of all donors with contact details).

2.Label & envelopes - name tags, address labels and address envelopes

3.One or two page contact summary - such as a donor, person or organisation profile.

4.Statistical graphical summaries and dashboards - analytic fast and easy to read visual display of your most important data.

5.Customised exports - choose and format data from Devman and create a file in spreadsheet format (Excel/CSV).

 

"Search and Report" menus are conveniently placed for easy access.
