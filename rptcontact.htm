Printing contact reports

 Top 

Overview

 

Development Manager provides two menu choices for printing reports: Search (simple) and Search advanced.

In both simple and advanced the steps are the same:

•select (filter) the data

•preview the result

•print the report in HTML( on screen), PDF or MS-Word to paper.

 

We will explain the simple method here. More sophisticated reporting is dealt with in Advanced reports.

 

Topics

 

       Go to People (or Organisation) > Search.

 

 

Note: Search options displayed may differ, depending on which options your organisation chose when the database was setup.

 

Note: You may want to print all the available information in the database. In that case you may simply proceed by clicking "Search". Sometimes you may want to print only a specific set. Make your search choices from the drop-down boxes.

 

Select your contact(s) and choose Print.

 

 

1.Development Manager provides many report options. As you make a selection you will see the options change intuitively.  In the Print or export contact lists screen, select a report type : Report; Label; Envelope or an export.
 

 

 

2.When you do this the other options will change according to your selection.

 

3.Click now on the one you've chosen: Report; Label; Envelope or Export.
