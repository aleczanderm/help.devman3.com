Simple single record lookup

When you want to find a single record use the simple search found on each page where it's needed:-

1. Home page; 2. People; 3. Organisations; 4. Initiatives (Project and Bursaries).

The search page displays differently depending on the page you came from, but the basics of searching for a record are the same.

Enter the name, part of the name or acronym of your contact in a Search box and select the contact from the drop-down menu.

To select one of the entities on the list that appears simply click in the appropriate row in the grid.

 

When working with reports and dashboards you will want to filter on a group of records.

 

1. You can search for any contact directly from the home page

 

2. Search for a person or organisation.
