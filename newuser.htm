When using Development Manager for the first time you may need to make some adjustments to your browser settings.

DevMan also allows for a number of global settings (for all your DevMan users) and personal settings (your own).

 

If you are unable to make these changes and would like assistance call your IT or the DevMan Support Team.

 

N.B.:  DevMan is fully tested for the three most widely used browsers.

The program may run on other browsers but we do not guarantee performance at this time.

Goto FAQ - Which browser for more information on this subject.

 

Before you get started with DevMan a short workshop is needed where we assist you in deciding such things as:

 

•Features to be enabled appropriate to your organisation

•Work-flow components to stream-line your daily tasks

•Appearance customising i.e. including your logo into Devman

•Communication (email and letter) templates

•Data take-on from legacy systems and spreadsheets
