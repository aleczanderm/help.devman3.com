View an organisation profile

 

Once you've entered an organisation's contact information this page allows you to review and change its details.

Here on a single screen is a summary of an organisation's important data, including its finances (donations, commitments, tax certificates),  its people and a useful history of your interactions with them, including reminders, correspondence, progress reports, events and mail-shots.  From this screen you can also link to all the proposals and projects, historic and current including their status.

 

(If you prefer a summarised presentation of the Organisation's information go to View > Summary for a view that may be more suited to you).

(If you want to make this your standard or default view, click here to see how ).
