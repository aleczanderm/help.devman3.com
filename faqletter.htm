       How do I create a new letter template?

 

When Development Manager was first setup a Kidz representative created a complete set of templates for you.

 

You can add more templates or change the wording or format of existing templates.

 

It is an advanced task to do this and best left to us to do it for you.

 

Setup a new letter In MS-Word with the wording and formatting as you would like it.

 

Send this to us at Kidz.

 

We will add the necessary tags and test it to see that it works as it should.

 

 

       My letter template is missing or has been incorrectly changed?

 

Letter templates are saved on the database and will not go missing.  Most likely it has been deleted or altered by another user.

 

You may correct the template wording and save the changed template.

 

We suggest that you limit the permissions to templates (see security) so that only one or two persons can make alterations to them.

 

If you have any difficulty at any time please contact Kidz by telephone or email at support@kidzafrica.co.za.
