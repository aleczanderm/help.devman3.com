You may find there are more than one record on Devman for an organisation. What can you do about this?

In addition you may have allocated a project to an incorrect organisation.

You can always delete the project and it's financials, and then enter a new project in the correct organisation.

 

Screenshot showing duplicates

 

These steps are obviously tedious, and to save you time you can simply reallocate an organisation and it's project.

Here are the steps for this much easier procedure.

 

 

1. Open the organisation (the wrong one i.e. whose projects you want to reallocate) and goto "Maintenance --> Reallocate

 

 

Maintenance --> Reallocate to cleanup

 

 

2. Click on "change" to find the correct organisation on Devman.

 

Look up the correct organisation to compare them.

 

3. You can now "reallocate" the wrong one to the correct one.

 

Reallocation has been successful

 

4. Now delete the wrong organisation
 

Delete duplicate organisation as it's associated info has been reallocated.
