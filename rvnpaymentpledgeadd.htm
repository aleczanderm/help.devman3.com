Add a pledge (grantmaking)

 Top 

The most effective way of managing your budget is to add pledges to your approved projects, especially when working with large amounts of money. This is especially useful when more than one payment will be made for a project.  Before adding a pledge ensure that a valid budget has been captured on the system.

 

1.Search for your approved project.

 

2.In the Project summary screen, go to Add > Pledge > Once off.

 

Click here to view:

 

3.Alternatively,in the Project summary screen, first view the pledges already made. Go to View > Financials > Pledges.

 

Click here to view

 

4.Click on New. To split the payment into installments, you can click Add installment plan.

 

Click here to view

 

5.In the Pledge details screen, choose which budget owner the money is to be paid from, the Budget period and category. (Once you have selected this information, each time you capture a new pledge the system will default to these choices, unless you change them).  In the description field you may include any information you feel is relevant to the pledge.  

 

6.Enter a date, and the amount you have approved for this project.
 

7.You can categorise your pledges (and therefore your payments) by Payment type e.g. Cash, Non-cash products & services, In kind, Discounts, vouchers etc.

 

8.You can indicate what payment method you will use e.g. EFT, cheque.

 

Click here to view

 

 

9.Once you have captured your pledge information, choose Save > Save and close.  If you wish to allocate a payment to this pledge, choose Save > Save and stay on page.
 

10.Once you have added your pledge, the Finance summary on the Project profile page will automatically update to include the pledge.
