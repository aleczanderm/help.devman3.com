       First time users

When DevMan is new for your organisation we will have worked with you to discuss some system settings.

We will also arrange for some basic training sessions where we will hold classroom sessions with your user team.

You may also request copies of our training guides or download them online.

 

If that's been done go directly to the logon page to enter your user name and password.

 

When you log into the program you will be presented with a default home page that suits most users.

The program is intuitive and flexible, so the interface may also be a little different depending on such

factors as:

•Preferences you have made.

•Your level of rights and permissions.

•Your company or department has requested features to be enabled.

 

Enter your User-ID and password.

Once the security has checked your credentials you will be allowed into the program and onto the Home page.
