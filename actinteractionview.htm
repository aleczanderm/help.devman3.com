View, edit and print an interaction

 Top 

 

1.To view a past interaction, search for and select your person, organisation or project.
Follow the steps below.

 

On the profile screen

From the contact or project go to View > Activity > Interactions.

 

2.All interactions with your organisation, person or project will display in the grid as shown here.

Interactions grid

If a number of interactions have been recorded, filter on your interactions by entering your name in the By drop-down.

 

3.To view the interaction click on the relevant row of the grid.

Click on the interaction to see the details

From the interaction details screen, you can make changes, create a reminder or print the details.
