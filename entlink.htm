It's important to know the relationship between persons, organisations and projects.

Devman allows for contacts to be linked to each other in many-to-many relationships. For example: organisational hierarchies, organisational affiliations, subsidiaries, personal relationships such as family and friends, etc.

 

A link is automatically established between an employer and an employee when you choose the organisation a person belongs to, and between a beneficiary and the benefactor.  However there may be other important relationships that you would like to show on the system.  For example, you can link a number of project managers to a project, as well as link two or more organisations as project partners.
