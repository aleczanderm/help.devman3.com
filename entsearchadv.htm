4. In addition to searching for a person's name you can search for an ID number, an email address, a postal or physical address by selecting an option from the Search field drop-down menu.  

Note: When searching for an organisation, you can search on NGO, NPO numbers and also a registration number.

6. To see your contact's record, simply click on the appropriate row in the grid.  

 

Note: You can send a text message (SMS) or an email to your contact(s) directly from the search page. Tick the white block next to the contact(s), and choose Send > SMS / Email from the menu. You can optionally choose Add Selection > To a new event / mailshot. to add the contact(s) to a new mailshot or event.
