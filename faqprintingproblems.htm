       Is there one easy place with all the printing options?

 

No  there isn't and for a good reason but yes it's quite easy to use.

Devman has many reports and these are accessible from all the places where it's needed.

On each of the high-level menus you will find three report sub-menus.

 

       Why can't I print?

 

You attempted to print a report using HTML, MS-Word and PDF and it doesn't display on the screen.

 

Since HTML is the default or basic method of reporting in a Web-based application,  then PDF and Word will probably also fail.

 

If this is a new problem (i.e. you have been able to print from Development Manager before) then first call your IT department to see if there is some underlying problem with your PC.  If they are unable to resolve the problem, call or email the Kidz Africa Team.

       Why can't I print to PDF?

 

When attempting to print to PDF you may get no further than one of the screens below.   This is most likely related to Adobe Reader installed on your PC.

 

Please determine if a version of Adobe Acrobat reader is actually installed and is working correctly.

 

It may be that there is an incomplete installation of Adobe that must be finished. (perhaps the license has not been accepted.)

 

If you have an older version of Adobe then it would be worthwhile to uninstall and install the latest version available.

 

 

 

       What must I do when it says "Load Report Failed"?

 

You may get this error when attempting to print to either PDF or MS-Word.

This problem is usually caused by a restriction in the environment the Web browser on your PC and can be fixed by you.

 

Follow these steps:-

 

1.Open the Web browser by clicking on Tools and selecting Internet Options.

 

Internet Options - To check security levels

 

 

 

2.Go to Security options.

 

 

3.Go to Custom level for Trusted sites options.

 

 

4.Scroll-down to Downloads-File download and select Enable option.

 

 

5.Save the setting and do your report again.
