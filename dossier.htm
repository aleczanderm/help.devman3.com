Dossier


Contents

 

Simplify your admin

With DevMan's dossier feature where you attach any document or file to an activity, organisation, project or contact person record.

 

There may be any number (or type) of files attached to a record in the Devman database such as:-

•electronic attachment from an email

•photo

•map

•scanned paper.

 

No separate folders to maintain on someone's pc

No longer do you need to maintain a list of folders where you keep important documents.

 

Whatever it is, it can be kept right where you need it, and you need no longer maintain them in separate folders on your database. Once uploaded they're actually in the database - safe and easily accessible.
