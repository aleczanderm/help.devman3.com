Print a budget report

 Top 

1.Go to Finance > Budgeting > Reports.

 

2.The Budget summary report will show what funds have been disbursed and what funds are still available.  You may choose to view (All) Budget types, owners, periods and categories. Or you may choose to view more specific information e.g. for the current financial year.

 

3.Once you have made your selections, click Print > Microsoft Word, Adobe Acrobat (pdf) or HTML.  Here is a sample of a Budget summary printed to MS-Word.
