Failed email/SMS communications

 Top 

 

To re-queue failed email and SMS batches follow these instructions:

 

 

1.From the Main DevMan menu select Administration > Communication log > Batch summaries

 

2.Click on the mail-shot with failed transmissions you want to re-queue.

 

Click here to view

 

3.To reduce the list change the "Status" to filter on the failed records only.

 

Click here to view

 

4.Next hover your cursor over "Update to … Pending" menu item.
 

 

5.You are asked “Do you want to manually re-queue these failed records?” select Yes
 

 

 

 

6.Your choice is confirmed and the selected failed batch of transmissions are re-queued
